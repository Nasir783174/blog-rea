How to Calculate CGPA

The CGPA formula is:

Sum of (Grade Point × Credits) ÷ Total Credits

Convert CGPA to Percentage

Convert CGPA to percentage using the right formula for your scale:

  • CBSE: CGPA × 9.5
  • AICTE: (CGPA − 0.75) × 10
  • US 4.0 scale: (CGPA ÷ 4.0) × 100

Manual CGPA Calculation

Learn how to calculate CGPA manually in 3 easy steps:

  1. Multiply grade points by credit hours.
  2. Sum the totals.
  3. Divide by total credits.
